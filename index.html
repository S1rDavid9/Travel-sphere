Need Help Finding Airport Codes?

IATA codes are 3-letter codes that represent airports worldwide. Some common examples:

  • JFK - John F. Kennedy International Airport, New York
  • LHR - Heathrow Airport, London
  • LAX - Los Angeles International Airport
  • CDG - Charles de Gaulle Airport, Paris

You can find airport codes online or check airline websites.
